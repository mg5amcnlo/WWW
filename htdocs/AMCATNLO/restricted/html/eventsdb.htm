Event samples database:

From this page you can get samples of events at NLO accuracy (in the LHE format) obtained within the MadGraph5_aMC@NLO framework that are ready for showering by a parton shower program. Click on any link to open a page with the process description and the links to the event files.

  • Higgs boson pair production in gluon fusion

  • Higgs boson production in vector boson fusion

  • Diphoton production in the ADD extra dimensions model

  • Single-top t-channel production in the four- and five-flavour scheme

  • W+1j production and W+2j production

  • Four-lepton production at the LHC

  • Z-boson production in association with a bottom-antibottom pair

  • W-boson production in association with a bottom-antibottom pair

  • Scalar and pseudoscalar Higgs production in association with a top-antitop pair
